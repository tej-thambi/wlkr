the problem


Trustworthy petcare is few and far between, and it’s hard to find petcare in any capacity. Whether you work long shifts or you take long vacations, the need for petcare is almost inevitable. Pets are our loved ones, our closest companions; they’re family. The care they require must reach a certain standard, and this standard has to be consistent. These issues are amplified for owners with non-traditional pets, like lizards, birds, or insects. While similar platforms may exist, none cater to pets, especially exotic ones, like wlkr plans to. Being a pet owner is difficult enough, and wlkr is here to help.

the wlkr solution


the wlkr app connects pet owners in need of pet care with qualified pet sitters in their area. our vision is to create a platform that supports niche pet owners while providing an interactive hub, linking pet owners with reliable caretakers and fellow owners.


profile


users complete a heavily customizable profile
providing wlkr with necessary information
to match clients and providers

search


wlkr completes a comprehensive search
based off parameters such as price, location, and qualifications,
giving pet owners several viable caretaker options

chat


owners connect with caretakers to
communicate their needs or with fellow owners
to get advice and recommendations

about


wlkr is a startup created by five like-minded entrepreneurs. Our team has worked tirelessly over the course of 10 weeks, during a UCLA incubator, to build wlkr. We worked through an ideation process, completed comprehensive customer and market research, forged through the development phase, and then created a pitch deck. Our hard work culminated during a presentation of our product to a panel of successful startup founders. We are extremely proud of our progress, and even more excited to bring wlkr to users around the world.
