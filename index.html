wlkr

the problem

pet owners face a variety of issues when searching for pet care in any capacity, either during a work shift, work trip, or vacation.
for example, finding consistent pet care that provides pets with a routine, or finding trustworthy and qualified pet sitters.
these issues are amplified for owners with non-traditional pets, like lizards, birds, or insects.
while similar platforms exist, none cater to exotic pets like wlkr plans to.

the wlkr solution

the wlkr app connects pet owners in need of pet care with qualified pet sitters in their area. our vision is to create a platform that supports niche pet owners while providing an interactive hub, linking pet owners with reliable caretakers and fellow owners.

profile

users complete a heavily customizable profile providing wlkr with necessary information to match clients and providers

search

wlkr completes a comprehensive search based off parameters such as price, location, and qualifications, giving pet owners several viable caretaker options

chat

pet owners connect with caretakers to communicate their needs, and owners can speak with fellow owners to get advice and recommendations

about

wlkr is a startup developed in the BruinLabs startup program at UCLA. our team worked over the course of 10 weeks to build wlkr.
we worked through an ideation process, a customer and market research process, an MVP development process, and a pitching process.
this culminated in a demo day where we pitched our product to fellow participants along with successful startup founders and received feedback.
