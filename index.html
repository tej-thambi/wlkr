wlkr

the problem

trustworthy petcare is few and far between, and it’s hard to find petcare in any capacity.
whether you work long shifts or you take long vacations, the need for petcare is almost inevitable. pets are our loved ones, they’re family., the care they require must reach a certain standard, and this care must be consistent.
these issues are amplified for owners with non-traditional pets, like lizards, birds, or insects.
thile similar platforms may exist, none cater to pets, especially exotic ones, like wlkr plans to.
being a pet owner is difficult enough, and wlkr is here to help.

the wlkr solution

the wlkr app connects pet owners in need of pet care with qualified pet sitters in their area. our vision is to create a platform that supports niche pet owners while providing an interactive hub, linking pet owners with reliable caretakers and fellow owners.

profile

users complete a heavily customizable profile providing wlkr with necessary information to match clients and providers

search

wlkr completes a comprehensive search based off parameters such as price, location, and qualifications, giving pet owners several viable caretaker options

chat/forum

pet owners connect with caretakers to communicate their needs, and owners can speak with fellow owners to get advice and recommendations

about

wlkr is a startup created by five like-minded entrepreneurs. our team has worked tirelessly over the course of 10 weeks, during a UCLA incubator, to build wlkr.
we worked through an ideation process, a customer and market research process, a development process, and created a comprehensive pitch deck.
our hard work culminated during a Demo Day, when we pitched our product to a panel of successful startup founders.
