pet owners

how do we verify petsitters?


We understand that the people who take care of your pets must be experienced and trustworthy.
This is a process we are still workshopping, but we plan on interviewing each candidate,
vetting them before they can become a verified sitter. We will also have badges that indicate
sitters have prior experience with a specific type of animal, whether it be as a previous owner
or in some other capacity. Additionally, our review system will help showcase past performance
and help build trust within our community.



what kinds of animals does wlkr cover?


If it exists, then wlkr most likely covers it. More traditional pets like cats and dogs are
covered. We also provide sitters/specialists for more exotic pets. If your pet isn't covered
in our system, you will have the option to make a special request to add it. We are here to
provide our clients with the best possible petcare, regardless of what that pet is, or what that
care may look like.



how will price be calculated?


Here at wlkr we understand how stressful petcare can be. To help alleviate some of that stress
we are experimenting with an affordable pay system. The exact numbers of our pricing is still in
the works. There will be a base fee and a per day fee, with add-ons for special requests and exotic animals.
Tip will be at the discretion of the pet owner based on exceptional performance. wlkr will only take
a small portion of the base fee, ensuring payment reaches the sitters who are working hard for your pets.
A full plan for our compensation system will be released at a later date.

pet sitters

how can i begin the hiring process?


Just reach out to us on any of our socials, call us, or e-mail us. Our Contact Us page should have all
the information you need. After your initial message, we will get back to you within 1-3 business days
Then we will initiate the interview process and look into your background with animals. If everything
checks out, then you can create your profile and begin accepting jobs!



how can i earn skill/verification badges?


During the interview process, you can demonstrate your experience with certain animals. For example
if you have worked with snakes in some capacity, you can provide us with proof or a reference, and
we will add this badge to your profile. If you still want to add badges after the interview process,
you can apply for a badge on the website with evidence or a reference. We will review your application
and add the new badge to your profile. The more badges the better!



how will i communicate with pet owners?


Our application will provide you with a chat feature where you can speak to your clients one-on-one.
You will also be able to post on our forums and gain clients via that platform. Initial client commmunication
will be conductly only on our app. After that, it is recommended that you speak with
clients on whatever platform both parties agree on when you are completing the actual job.
